Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a "character"?

A character is any letter, number, or punctuation mark in the text you submit for audio generation. Spaces are also counted as characters. You only use characters when you successfully generate audio.

Can I upgrade or cancel anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade your plan instantly from your dashboard, and characters will be prorated. You can cancel your subscription at any time, and you'll retain access until the end of your current billing cycle.

Do I own the rights to the generated audio?

With the Creator and Studio plans, you retain full commercial rights to all audio generated on the platform. The Starter plan is strictly for personal, non-commercial use only.

How secure is my cloned voice?

Extremely secure. Voice clones are encrypted and tied strictly to your account. We never use your cloned voice data to train our public models or allow other users to access it without your explicit API permission.
