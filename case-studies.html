Nexus Studios

AAA Game Developer

Voicing a Universe Without Limits.

The Challenge: For their upcoming open-world RPG, Nexus Studios needed over 10,000 unique lines of dialogue for background NPCs. Traditional studio recording would have taken months and cost hundreds of thousands of dollars.

The Solution: Using VoxForge's API, the audio team cloned 20 baseline voices from their core cast. By programmatically tweaking the pitch, speed, and emotional parameters (Stability & Clarity sliders), they generated 100 distinct character variations.

The Daily Pulse

Top 100 News Podcast

Going Global overnight.

The Challenge: Expanding a daily US news podcast to international markets. Re-recording episodes daily in multiple languages with native speakers was logistically impossible and financially prohibitive.

The Solution: The Daily Pulse team used VoxForge's multi-language translation engine. They cloned the main host's voice once. Now, scripts are automatically translated and generated in Spanish, French, German, and Japanese, retaining the host's exact pacing and tone.
