General Inquiries

The Basics

What exactly is VoxForge AI?

VoxForge AI is an advanced neural text-to-speech (TTS) and voice cloning platform. It allows creators to generate incredibly lifelike voiceovers from text or clone their own voices using just a short audio sample.

How much audio do I need to clone a voice?

For the best results, we recommend a clean, high-quality audio recording of about 60 to 90 seconds without any background noise, music, or echo. The system can work with less, but more varied emotional range in the sample yields a better clone.

Can I use generated voices in commercial projects?

Yes, provided you are on the Creator or Studio plan. Free Starter plans are strictly restricted to personal, non-monetized use. If you upgrade, you gain full commercial rights to the audio generated with our pre-made library or your own cloned voices.

How many languages are supported?

Our v2 engine currently supports text-to-speech and voice cloning across 50+ languages, including English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Mandarin, Hindi, and many more, with proper dialect and accent preservation.

Pricing & Subscriptions

Billing Questions

What counts as a "character" towards my quota?

A character is any letter, number, or punctuation mark in the text you submit. Spaces are also counted. Characters are only deducted from your monthly quota when you successfully click "Generate" and produce an audio file.

Do unused characters roll over to the next month?

No, monthly character quotas do not roll over. Your quota resets completely on the first day of your new billing cycle. If you run out, you can optionally purchase "Top-Up Credits" from your dashboard which never expire.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime by navigating to Dashboard > Billing > Manage Subscription. Once cancelled, you will not be billed again, but you will retain access to your premium features until the end of your current paid billing period.

Technical & API

Technical Support

What are the API rate limits?

API rate limits depend on your tier. Starter plans do not have API access. Creator plans allow 30 requests per minute. Studio plans allow 100 requests per minute. Enterprise users can request custom rate limits.

What file formats are exported?

By default, generations are provided as 128kbps MP3s. Paid plans unlock higher bitrates (192kbps and 320kbps) as well as uncompressed WAV formats. The API can stream audio in PCM format.

Why does my cloned voice sound slightly robotic?

This usually happens if the training sample had background noise, echoes, or if the speaker spoke in a monotone voice. Try deleting the clone and uploading a new 60-second clip recorded in a quiet room, speaking clearly with dynamic emotion.
